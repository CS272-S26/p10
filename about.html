About Us

Who We Are

We are a student built mock store platform focused on creating a simple, fast, and easy shopping experience. Our goal is to make product discovery effortless and enjoyable.

Our Mission

Our mission is to (lose as little points as possible on the project) simplify online shopping by combining RainForestApi for scraping Amazon, simple js, and a seamless cart and checkout experience. We believe shopping should be quick and user friendly.

Our Guarantee

We ensure accurate product data and smooth browsing (of course with a few seconds of delay thatis) and a reliable checkout process (don't worry it doesn't actually checkout anything). this is a CMS project, so it is my guarantee that nothing you buy will actually arrive, so start browsing right now to get the products you have always wanted!
